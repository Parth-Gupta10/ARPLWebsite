MBTS
Rubber Accelerator 		MBTS is a semi-ultra fast accelerator for NR, SBR, BR NBR and other highly unsaturated rubbers. It readily disperses in rubber compounds and acts as a retarder for CR based compounds (depending on type of CR used). MBTS is a retarder of cure in rubber compounds accelerated using thiurams (TMTM/TMTD) class accelerators as main accelerator and reduces bloom of thiurams to a great extent.

MBTS has a long curing range and is safer than MBT at rubber compound processing tempera-tures. MBTS is very active above curing temperature of 140°C giving flat curing characteristics to 'polyi-soprene' rubbers (e.g. NR). MBTS accelerated compounds exhibit very good tensile and resilience properties when used in combination with other booster accelerators.		 Light Yellow Powder 170-175

CBS
Rubber Accelerator 		CBS is a delayed action sulphenamide accelerator for NR, SBR, BR, NBR and other sulphur / sulphur donor cured diene rubbers. CBS is supplied in dust free pellet form which offers advantages such as easy handling, accurate weighing on all types of weighing systems. CBS pellets disperse easily in rubber compounds. CBS is also available in oil treated powder form.

Normal dosages of Zinc oxide and Stearic acid are necessary for all sulfenamide accelerators. However, excess dosage of Stearic acid does not offer additional advantage as in case of thiazoles. The normal dosage of CBS is in the range of 0.5-1.0 phr for conventional cure systems, 1.5-2.0 phr for Semi-EV cures and 3.0-5.0 phr for low sulphur EV cures. CBS is used at 1.0 phr dosage as an effective retarder for sulphur-less cures based on thiuram accelerators. 		Off white powder 97-105

MBT
Rubber Accelerator 		MBT is a semi ultra fast accelerator for NR, SBR, BR, NBR and other highly unsaturated diene rubbers. It readily disperses in rubber compounds and also acts as a mild peptizer for NR & CR. MBT is a re-tarder of cure in rubber compounds accelerated using thiurams (TMTD/TMTM) / dithiocarbamates (ZDC, ZDBC) or ETU class accelerators as main accelerators. Even relatively small dosages of MBT show significant effect and reduce bloom of thiurams and dithiocarbamates. MBT also acts as a retarder in CR based compounding formulations.

MBT has a long curing range and is very active above 115°C. MBT gives flat curing characteris-tics for 'polyisoprene' rubber (e.g. NR) based compounds exhibiting ability to withstand long over cures. However, MBT accelerator is scorchy in nature and the rate and the state of cure achieved is comparatively lower than sulphenamide accelerators like CBS & TBBS. Thus, when used alone, MBT exhibits comparatively lower tensile properties and resilience. However, in combination with other accelerators marked improvements are observed. TMTD
Rubber Accelerator 		TMT is an ultra fast accelerator for NR, SBR, BR, NBR and other highly unsaturated rubbers and most preferred primary accelerator for sulphur cured low unsaturation content rubbers like Butyl rubber (IIR) and EPDM.

TMT is widely used as a secondary accelerator with thiazole / sulphenamide class accelerators to achieve faster curing rate and higher cross link density with a compromise on scorch safety. TMT contains approx. 13% available sulphur for cross linking and hence, can be used in low sulphur / sulphur less EV cures of diene rubbers. TMT does not liberate ‘free sulphur’ during sulphur less cures and the vulcanizates exhibit excellent heat and compression set resistance. 		White Powder 142-147
degree celsius
ZMBT
Rubber Accelerator 		ZMBT is a semi-ultra fast accelerator for NR, SBR, NBR, BR and other diene rubbers.

The critical cure temperature of ZMBT is approx. 120°C and dry rubber / latex based compounds are vulcanized quickly at 125°C.The state of cure developed by ZMBT is slightly lower than MBT; however, scorch safety is much higher and hence can be used in place of MBT for scorch control.

ZMBT is mainly used in latex compounding along with ZDC/ ZDBC to improve the state of cure. ZMBT improves the ageing characteristics of NR latex based vulcanizates and does not affect the stability of NR latex based compounds.		 Off White Powder N/A
ZDEC
Rubber Accelerator 		ZDC is a widely used ultra fast accelerator for NR latex based compounds and also finds applications as primary or secondary accelerator in most dry rubber based sulphur cured compounds.

ZDC is very active at temperatures above 100°C. As a primary accelerator in NR dry rubber based compounds. ZDC exhibits scorching tendency, faster cure rate, higher cross link density but poor reversion resistance. However; ZDC does not affect the stability of NR latex based compounds. TBBS
Rubber Accelerator 		TBBS is a delayed action sulphenamide accelerator for NR, SBR, BR, NBR and other sulphur / sulphur donor cured diene rubbers. TBBS exhibits very good storage stability as compared to other sulphenamide accelerators and TBBS pellets disperse easily in rubber compounds.

F-Powder
Rubber Accelerator 		- F is a semi-ultra fast accelerator blend for NR, SBR, BR, NBR and other highly unsaturated diene rubbers. It readily disperses in rubber compounds, and has been specially formulated to avoid grit formation on prolonged storage. F has a long curing range and is very active above 115°C. F gives fast curing characteristics for polyisoprene rubber (e.g. NR) based compounds. At equal dosages in NR, F is less scorchy than MBT and exhibits almost similar or slightly faster cure rate and higher state of cure as compared to MBT or MBTS and hence the stress-strain properties of the vulcanizates are also superior. Thus, F can be used as a single accelerator for rubber compounds where faster curing rates are desired. Cream powder 140 degree celsius
